Family Outing on the Horse farm

Come out to Carl's horse farm (Shallow Hill), bring the kids and have a relaxing afternoon.

What you'll get

A full weekend afternoon with 9 other families on the farm. Carl will provide a home cooked meal for lunch (or dinner depending on timing / people's preferences). Everyone will get a chance to pet, feed and ride a horse in a calm, quiet environment.

Price

  • $100 per family, up to 3 people.
  • $25 a person for additional family members. Children must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian1.

Details

  • Everyone will have a chance to meet, pet, brush and ride a horse. There will be plenty of students on hand to help lead pony rides.
  • Event will last 4-5 hours and include a home cooked meal and drinks.
  • We will take lots of pictures and have them available for you to download and take home or post to our Facebook wall
