Hot Pocket Fundraiser

In 2012 one of my co-workers (you know who you are) left a Hot Pocket (with crisper) in the microwave for way too long. The result was both hilarious and disaserous (the area by the microwave, which I happen to sit directly by, reeked of smoke for days). All in all it was pretty impressive.

Let's burn some Hot Pockets
(and raise money for charity)

Families shouldn't have to decide between paying the rent and buying food. The Mid-Ohio Food Bank provides emergency food assistance to more than 248,000 individuals, including 86,975 children and 17,395 seniors1. To that end, I'll give you what you want to see Internet - I'll microwave that hotpocket. I'll microwave the crap out of it2 and video tape it for you and upload it on the internet.

The catch

For every $50 I raise I'll microwave that Hot Pocket, in it's toaster sleeve, for one minute. So $250 = 5 minutes (= booooring), but $1,000 = 20 minutes (= guaranteed fun time).
